ALOE VERA
This plant needs very little care. Aloe vera withstands dry weather very well.
The plant should be watered well occasionally, and then allowed to dry. It is much better for the plant than to give it a little water regularly. It is important that the water can drain and not stay on the bottom of the pot. This houseplant likes a lot of light and can even stand to be placed in direct sunlight.

FICUS LYRATA
Fiddle-leaf fig or Ficus lyrata has its name derived from the resemblance of the leaves to a violin (fiddle). Originally from the African rainforests, this ficus is an amazing decorative plant due to its unique, easily recognizable foliage. It loves light and needs to be rotated every few weeks to grow in a balanced way
